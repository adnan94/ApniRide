Honda Aspire

Honda Pakistan is back again after a long time with its much awaited new versions of Honda City 2015. Recently Honda City announced its six models that are much better than previous models. Look is more sporty than before and width, height and length are also larger than previous models. A complete killing machine by the Honda. In new models there is i-VTEC Engine Technology that results in improving the volumetric efficiency of a four-stroke internal combustion engine. It is very different from normal VVT which advances the valve timing only but does not change the camshaft profile or valve lift in any way. However, Honda has take control on all these issues by introducing i-VTEC in its latest models. Check what Honda City brings for you after 2011.
