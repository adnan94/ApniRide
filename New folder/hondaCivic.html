Honda Civic (2015)

The last two versions of civic are the most luxuriant ones, giving you the convenience of automatic transmission as well as navigation. Finding your way to required destination will never be a difficult task now. Plus, you will never find yourself lost as the navigation system will always help you locate your current position. All these seven trims of Honda Civic possess the same basic specifications for their engine. This 1.8 L standard engine is capable of producing 140 horsepower thrust. The highway mileage is 36 mpg while the city mileage is up to 28 mpg. What car lovers like most about this majestic vehicle is its fuel economy as well as the plush interior. Luxury is the word which describes the interior of Civic in the most comprehensive way. Moreover, it is perfectly quiet inside with no disturbing sounds from the car’s engine spoiling your peaceful trip. The car has sufficient room for three passengers at the rear seating arrangement, making the total seating capacity equal to five. Civic is available in six amazing colors. Albaster Silver and Taffeta White add to the sophistication and elegance of the car. On the other hand, the colors like Crystal Black and Carnelian Red compliment the vehicle’s magnificence. The lustrous headlights flanking the front grille further add beauty to the cars overall appearance as you can see in Honda Civic pictures.
